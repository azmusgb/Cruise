Cruise Pro Tips & Recommendations

Expert advice for the perfect Caribbean cruise experience • Adventure of the Seas • February 14–20, 2026

Priority Tips (Must-Do)

These tips can make or break your cruise experience

Royal App is Essential

Download before you sail. Use for daily schedules, onboard chat, and account monitoring. Enable notifications for important announcements.

  • Check-in digitally before arrival
  • View daily activity schedules
  • Message family for free onboard
  • Monitor SeaPass account charges
Must Do Free Onboard

Packing Essentials

Mid-Feb temps: 75-82°F days, 68°F nights. Pack light layers, reef-safe SPF 50, quick-dry swimwear, water shoes, and two formal outfits.

  • Passports valid beyond Aug 2026
  • Magnetic hooks for schedules (walls are metal)
  • USB-C hub for extra charging ports
  • Night-light for bathroom trips
  • Motion sickness remedies just in case
  • Non-surge power strip (surge protectors prohibited)

All Pro Tips & Recommendations

Expert advice for every aspect of your cruise

Family Programs

  • Adventure Ocean: Ages 3-12, supervised activities
  • Teen Lounge: Ages 13-17, video games, dances
  • Royal Babies & Tots: 6-36 months (reservation-only)
  • Splashaway Bay: Water play area for littles
  • In-room babysitting: Available evenings

Ship Time Critical

Always follow ship time, not local port time. Set watches to ship time before leaving ship to avoid missing departure.

Warning: Jamaica is in different time zone. Double-check ship time when ashore!
Critical

Photo Packages

  • Unlimited Digital Package: $249 (pre-cruise discount)
  • Video Recap: $149 if pre-ordered
  • Best photos: Formal nights (Day 2 & 5)
  • Portraits: Gangway, dining room, promenade
  • Digital only: No prints to carry home

Crown & Anchor Society

  • Earn 1 point per night (2 in suites)
  • Priority check-in at Gold level
  • Exclusive events at higher tiers
  • Diamond members: Free drinks 5-8 PM
  • Sign up: Free on Royal Caribbean website

Cabin Hacks

  • Magnetic hooks: For schedules, hats, lanyards
  • USB nightlight: For late bathroom trips
  • Close balcony curtains at night for privacy
  • Interior cabins: Use bridge cam channel on TV
  • Over-door organizer: For toiletries, small items
  • Ask steward for extra hangers, pillows

Deck Shortcuts

  • Deck 11: Pools, Windjammer buffet, Sky Bar
  • Deck 5: Royal Promenade (24/7 snacks, shops)
  • Deck 4: Medical center, Photo gallery
  • Deck 13: FlowRider® (early AM = no lines)
  • Deck 12: Fitness center, Spa
  • Forward elevators less crowded than midship

Budget Hacks

  • Free champagne at art auction preview
  • Shop clearance on Day 6 (last sea day)
  • Half-price specialty lunch on port days
  • Diamond members: Free drinks 5-8 PM
  • Bring own wine: 1 bottle per adult allowed
  • Port shopping: Better deals than onboard

Safety Prep

  • Passports in cabin safe when ashore
  • Closed-toe shoes for excursions
  • Carry $200 in small bills for tips, taxis
  • Photograph valuables before packing
  • Emergency contacts saved in phone
  • Travel insurance documents accessible

Dining Tips

  • Main Dining Room: Same table each night
  • Windjammer: Less crowded at opening/closing
  • Room service: Free continental breakfast
  • Specialty restaurants: Book first day for best times
  • Formal nights: Day 2 (sea) & Day 5 (sea)
  • Allergies: Notify staff first day

Port Day Tips

  • George Town: Tender port, early tender ticket
  • Falmouth: Walk to port area, safe shopping
  • CocoCay: Free tram, claim chairs early
  • Always carry: Ship card, ID, money, water
  • Return early: Avoid last-minute crowds
  • Local currency: USD accepted everywhere

Health & Wellness

  • Hand sanitizer: Use frequently, stations everywhere
  • Seasickness: Patches, pills, or wristbands
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water, especially in sun
  • Sun protection: Reef-safe SPF 50, reapply often
  • Medical center: Deck 1, 24/7 (fee applies)
  • Motion: Middle decks, midship cabins rock less

Connectivity

  • VOOM Wi-Fi: Surf + Stream from $16/day
  • Royal app chat: Free messaging onboard
  • Airplane mode: At sailaway to avoid roaming
  • Best signal: Deck 4 library, Promenade
  • In port: Local cafes often have free Wi-Fi
  • WhatsApp group: "RaheCruise2026" for updates
