Royal App is Essential

Critical

Download before you sail. The Royal Caribbean app is your cruise command center.

  • Digital check-in before arrival
  • View daily activity schedules
  • Free messaging with family onboard
  • Monitor SeaPass account charges
  • Complete mandatory eMuster safety briefing

Ship Time is Critical

Critical

Always follow ship time, not local port time. Set watches to ship time before leaving ship.

Warning: Jamaica is in different time zone. Double-check ship time when ashore!

Packing Essentials

Critical

Mid-Feb temperatures: 75-82°F days, 68°F nights. Don't forget these cruise must-haves:

  • Passports valid beyond August 2026
  • Magnetic hooks for schedules (walls are metal)
  • Non-surge power strip (surge protectors prohibited)
  • Reef-safe SPF 50+ sunscreen
  • Water shoes for Dunn's River Falls
  • Motion sickness remedies just in case

Family Programs & Kids

Family
  • Adventure Ocean: Ages 3-12, supervised activities included
  • Teen Lounge: Ages 13-17, video games, dances, hangout
  • Royal Babies & Tots: 6-36 months (reservation required)
  • Splashaway Bay: Water play area for little ones
  • In-room babysitting: Available evenings for extra fee

Dining Tips & Tricks

Dining
  • Main Dining Room: Lobster tail on formal nights (Day 2 & 5)
  • Specialty Dining: Half-price lunch on port days
  • Windjammer: Best times: 7-9 AM, 11:30-1 PM, 6-7 PM
  • Room Service: Continental breakfast free, $7.95 fee otherwise
  • Bring wine: 1 bottle per adult allowed onboard

Port Day Essentials

Ports
  • Grand Cayman (tender): Morning tenders busy, go after 10 AM
  • Jamaica: Water shoes REQUIRED for Dunn's River Falls
  • CocoCay: No passport needed, towels provided
  • Carry: Small bills for tips, taxi, souvenirs
  • All aboard: Be back 1 hour before departure
