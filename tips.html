Family Programs

Family
  • Adventure Ocean: Ages 3-12, supervised activities
  • Teen Lounge: Ages 13-17, video games, dances
  • Royal Babies & Tots: 6-36 months (reservation-only)
  • Splashaway Bay: Water play area for littles
  • In-room babysitting: Available evenings

Photo Packages

Memories
  • Unlimited Digital Package: $249 (pre-cruise discount)
  • Video Recap: $149 if pre-ordered
  • Best photos: Formal nights (Day 2 & 5)
  • Portraits: Gangway, dining room, promenade
  • Digital only: No prints to carry home

Crown & Anchor

Loyalty
  • Earn 1 point per night (2 in suites)
  • Priority check-in at Gold level
  • Exclusive events at higher tiers
  • Diamond members: Free drinks 5-8 PM
  • Sign up: Free on Royal Caribbean website

Cabin Hacks

Comfort
  • Magnetic hooks: For schedules, hats, lanyards
  • USB nightlight: For late bathroom trips
  • Over-door organizer: For toiletries, small items
  • Interior cabins: Use bridge cam channel on TV
  • Ask steward for extra hangers, pillows

Budget Hacks

Savings
  • Free champagne at art auction preview
  • Shop clearance on Day 6 (last sea day)
  • Half-price specialty lunch on port days
  • Bring own wine: 1 bottle per adult allowed
  • Port shopping: Better deals than onboard

Safety Prep

Important
  • Passports in cabin safe when ashore
  • Closed-toe shoes for excursions
  • Carry $200 in small bills for tips, taxis
  • Photograph valuables before packing
  • Emergency contacts saved in phone
