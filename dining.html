Additional Dining Information & Services

Dietary Needs & Allergies

Special accommodations available • Notify us in advance

Vegetarian & Vegan

Notify your server for special menus. Available in all restaurants. Vegan options clearly marked on menus.

Gluten-Free

Pre-order meals 24 hours in advance. Dedicated preparation area. Extensive gluten-free options available.

Food Allergies

Inform dining staff of any allergies. Chefs can prepare separate meals. Notify us during booking for best service.

Special Requests

Contact head waiter for special arrangements. Kosher/Halal meals available with 60 days advance notice.

Important Dining Information

  • Reservations: Specialty dining can be booked via Royal Caribbean app, cabin TV, or at restaurant podium. Pre-cruise booking recommended.
  • Cancellation Policy: 24-hour cancellation policy for specialty restaurants to avoid charges. No-shows may be charged full price.
  • Formal Nights: Professional photography available throughout ship on formal nights (typically Day 2 & 5).
  • Gratuities: Included in specialty dining packages. Additional tipping at guest discretion for exceptional service.
  • Children: Kids' menus available in Main Dining Room and Windjammer. Children under 12 dine at reduced price in specialty restaurants.
  • Special Diets: Notify Royal Caribbean of dietary restrictions at least 30 days before sailing for best accommodations.
  • Disclaimer: Dining venues, menus, pricing, and hours are subject to change without notice. Confirm details onboard.
