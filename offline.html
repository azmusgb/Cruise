Connection Troubleshooting

1

Check Wi-Fi

Ensure you're connected to the ship's Wi-Fi network "Royal-WiFi" or have cellular data enabled.

2

Airplane Mode

Toggle Airplane mode off and on to reset your device's network connections.

3

Restart App

Close and reopen the Royal Caribbean app to refresh the connection.

4

Move Locations

Try moving to a different area of the ship for better signal reception.
