Grand Cayman

Tender logistics reward early timing discipline.

  • Must-do: Tender early if you booked a fixed-time excursion.
  • Bring/Wear: Light layers, water shoes, small cash buffer.

Falmouth, Jamaica

Port town efficiency improves with a hard return-time plan.

  • Must-do: Set return alarm for 90 minutes before all-aboard.
  • Bring/Wear: Photo ID, ship card, sun protection, closed shoes.
  • Skip: Last-minute transport dependencies near departure.
