Deck Navigation Tips

Orientation Tip

Remember: Port (left) side has red accents, Starboard (right) has green. Use carpets and signage to navigate.

Elevator Strategy

Forward elevators are less crowded. Use stairs between decks 3-6 for quick access to dining and rooms.

Peak Times

Avoid main elevators during dinner hours (6-8 PM) and after shows. Use forward/aft elevators instead.

Walking Distances

Deck 6 connects to everything. It's the central hub - remember this when planning your activities.
